RLadies+ Global is run by a volunteer group with designated responsibilities within the organisation.
The leadership team provides overall direction — keeping the organisation well-structured, honouring commitments to sponsors and members, and guiding where RLadies+ grows next.

If you would like to join the global team, the [RLadies+ Guide](https://guide.rladies.org/about/globalteam/) covers what the work involves.
Take a look at our [current vacancies](https://airtable.com/appZjaV7eM0Y9FsHZ/shrDBOsyaFS28wOxZ) and fill out [the interest form](/form/global-team-application/) for any roles that appeal to you.
