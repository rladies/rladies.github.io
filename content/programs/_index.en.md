Most of the work within RLadies+ happens through the chapter organisers around the world.
The global organisation supports them with technical infrastructure and a network they can turn to for help and guidance.
The global team also coordinates activities that span all chapters — creating pathways to the international network, increasing visibility of RLadies+ actions, and spotlighting minority genders in tech and R.
